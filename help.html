How accurate are the transcripts?

+

Our AI-powered system achieves 99%+ accuracy for clear audio in supported languages. Accuracy may vary based on audio quality, background noise, accents, and speaking speed. We recommend reviewing and editing transcripts for critical applications.

Which languages are supported?

+

We support transcription in 50+ languages including: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Russian, Japanese, Chinese (Mandarin), Korean, Arabic, Hindi, and many more. The system automatically detects the language of the video.

How long does extraction take?

+

Most transcripts are extracted within 30-60 seconds, depending on video length and audio complexity. Longer videos (1+ hours) may take 2-3 minutes. The progress bar shows real-time processing status.

Is my data private and secure?

+

Absolutely. We process all transcripts securely and never store your video content or transcripts. All processing happens in real-time, and your data is automatically deleted after the session ends. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices.

Can I edit the transcript after extraction?

+

Yes! You can edit the transcript directly in our interface before exporting. Use the search function to find specific sections, make corrections, and then copy or export the edited version. Changes are not saved automatically, so make sure to export your edited transcript.

Is there a video length limit?

+

ScriptExtract can handle videos up to 3 hours in length. For longer content, we recommend breaking it into smaller segments. Most videos under 2 hours process quickly and efficiently.

Troubleshooting

Common issues and their solutions

‚ùå "Invalid URL" Error

Cause: The URL format is not recognized or the video is not accessible.

Solution:

  • Check that the URL starts with https://www.youtube.com/ or https://youtu.be/
  • Ensure the video is public and not private or deleted
  • Try copying the URL directly from the browser address bar
  • Remove any additional parameters after the video ID

‚ö†Ô∏è "No Audio Detected" Error

Cause: The video doesn't contain any recognizable speech or audio.

Solution:

  • Verify the video has audible speech or dialogue
  • Check that the video's audio isn't muted or corrupted
  • Try a different video to test if the issue is specific to one video
  • Ensure the video isn't primarily music without lyrics

üêå Slow Processing Times

Cause: Long videos, poor audio quality, or high server load.

Solution:

  • For videos longer than 1 hour, be patient - processing can take 2-3 minutes
  • Try processing during off-peak hours (early morning or late evening)
  • Check your internet connection speed
  • Consider breaking very long videos into shorter segments

üîç Poor Transcript Quality

Cause: Audio quality issues, background noise, or strong accents.

Solution:

  • Use videos with clear audio and minimal background noise
  • Manually edit the transcript using our built-in editor
  • For heavily accented speech, try videos with clearer pronunciation
  • Use high-quality headphones to better hear the original audio
