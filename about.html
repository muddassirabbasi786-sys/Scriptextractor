About ScriptExtract

We're revolutionizing how people work with YouTube content by making transcript extraction instant, accurate, and accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

In a world where video content dominates, we believe the spoken word should be as accessible as the written word. ScriptExtract bridges this gap by providing instant, accurate transcripts from any YouTube video.

Whether you're a content creator repurposing videos, a researcher analyzing interviews, a student taking notes, or a professional creating documentation, ScriptExtract empowers you to work more efficiently.

Our advanced AI technology ensures 99%+ accuracy while maintaining the natural flow and context of the original speech, saving you hours of manual transcription work.

Our simple 4-step process gets you from video to transcript in seconds

1

Paste URL

2

Our advanced AI analyzes the video's audio track and identifies speech patterns and language.
